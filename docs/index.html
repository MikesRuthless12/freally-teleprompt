In development — Windows, macOS & Linux. Free core, local-first, no subscription required.

  • A real reading pace

    Set speed in characters per second or by BPM (20–250) to read lyrics and verses in time with music — not an arbitrary 1–10 slider.

  • Pause chips (caesuras)

    Type -- for a beat or --N for an N-second hold; each becomes an atomic marker, lit one at a time as you read.

  • Preview = projector

    One character-based engine keeps the operator preview, the talent projector, and any LAN device in step — even at different fonts.

  • Autocomplete, offline

    Ghost-text word and phrase suggestions from bundled per-language dictionaries — 18 languages, instant, and entirely on your machine. Tab accepts, Esc dismisses.

  • Start countdown & read-aloud

    An optional 3-2-1 pre-roll on preview and projector, plus a read-aloud preview through your operating system's own speech engine.

  • Yours, privately

    Local-first and account-free — your scripts stay on your machine. No AI, no telemetry, nothing sent anywhere. Permissively licensed end to end.
