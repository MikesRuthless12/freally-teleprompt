The LAN mirror is still being built

This page is where a phone, tablet, or second laptop will join a running Freally Teleprompt session and mirror the script as it scrolls — in your browser, with nothing to install.

It is not wired up yet. When it ships, the desktop app will show you a link and a QR code for this page; opening it on any device on the same network is the whole setup.

What it will do

  • Mirror the scroll in step. The same character-based engine drives the operator preview, the talent projector, and this page, so they stay on the same word even at completely different font sizes.
  • Read comfortably on a small screen. Set your own font size here without changing what anyone else sees.
  • No install and no account — it is a web page on your own network.

Your script never leaves your network. The mirror is served by the desktop app directly to devices you point at it. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is relayed through a server, and there is no AI anywhere in Freally Teleprompt.

Meanwhile, the documentation covers what the app does today, and the changelog tracks each release.
